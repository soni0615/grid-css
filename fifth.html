Justify-Self:-justify-self apply individually to particular items.Its just used for aligning the items in horizontal direction.It is suitable for flex and grid both type of layouts.We can move in right,left,center direction horizontally using justify-self.This property works on items.

Justify-Items:-Justify-Items applies on the parent just on container level not just on particular items.It is suitable for flex and grid both type of layouts.It just defines the default justify-self items.It also works in horizontal direction.This property applies on all the items.
